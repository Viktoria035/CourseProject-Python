Rules

  • You can play a game based on different categories. Each category has max 10 questions.
  • If you answer a question correctly, you will get 10 points. If you answer a question incorrectly, you will get 0 points. If the question is with more than one correct answer, you will get only the points from the correct answer.
  • The questions shall be in the form of multiple choice and single choice.
  • You can play alone or with your friends in a group. You can also play with a random opponent.
  • After the game, you can see the leaderboard and your score.
